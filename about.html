About Goyal Fashions Private Limited

40+ Years of Excellence in Textile Manufacturing & Business Innovation

Our Legacy

We are clothing manufacturer managed by technically qualified professionals, being part of the flagship company called Goyal Fashions. The promoters have the background of extensive experience in the field of textiles & clothing business spanning over 4 decades.

Our organization has been a leading manufacturer/exporter for the last over 25 years, we have established a name for ourselves in the international market for quality and reliability. The group turnover of the company is about US$ 10 million, in which about 2.5 million contributes to garment business.

Our Objective

To fulfil the buyers and indirectly the final consumers requirement for quality and value over price. We provide to the buyer and further-on to the final consumer quality merchandise at economical price, a commitment based on mutual trust.

The company strives for consistent quality improvements on each level of its operation and has laid thrust on Development activities. On job training for workers is an integral part of its philosophy. Its Personal Department ensures safety and welfare of the employees at the prime agendas of the company to maintain a congenial working atmosphere compatible to the International Standards.

Infrastructure

Our factory in Jaipur cover an area of over 100,000 sq. Ft., with a 300 sewing machines set up, including embroidery, Kaj Buttoning, Washing, Finishing, Checking/QC and Packing.

It also houses the administrative office, designing and development and the Fabric/accessory stores.
