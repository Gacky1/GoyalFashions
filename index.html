Our Legacy

Founded on 6 March 1981, Goyal Fashions Private Limited is a reputable private company headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Incorporated under CIN U18101RJ1981PTC018803, the company has grown from its textile roots to become a flagship for a diverse group spanning textiles, hospitality, solar energy, and retail. With over four decades of leadership, our promoters— technically qualified professionals—have built a name synonymous with quality, consistency, and export excellence.
