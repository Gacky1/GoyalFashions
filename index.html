Our Legacy

Goyal Fashions Private Limited, a renowned name in the hospitality sector, is the proud parent company behind the esteemed chain of Ananta Hotels and Resorts. With a strong presence across premium hospitality destinations, Goyal Fashions has established itself as a leader in delivering world-class guest experiences. Beyond hospitality, the company is deeply committed to community development and skill empowerment. Through its dedicated skill training initiatives, Goyal Fashions Private Limited has consistently worked towards uplifting underprivileged and deserving youth by equipping them with practical, industry-relevant skills across various sectors. In 2024, Goyal Fashions Private Limited was officially empanelled with the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, under the Hunar Se Rozgar Tak (HSRT) scheme. As part of this initiative, the organization has currently trained 300 students in the hospitality sector, helping them pave the way towards gainful employment and self-reliance.
