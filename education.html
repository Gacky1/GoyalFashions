Ananta Institute of Hotel Management & Allied Studies (AIHMAS)

Where Hospitality Meets Opportunity

Founded in 2015

The Ananta Institute of Hotel Management & Allied Studies (AIHMAS) is a premier hospitality education institution operating under the Goyal Fashions education vertical. Affiliated with Rajasthan ILD Skills University and recognized by UGC, the institute has earned a reputation for academic excellence and practical training.

Campus: Spread over one acre with a WiFi-enabled, modern campus featuring well-equipped classrooms, labs, training kitchens, library, and hospitality simulation areas.

Our Skill Training Model

We follow a "Training-to-Employment" approach

Step 1: Industry-Centric Curriculum

Courses designed based on current industry requirements and standards to ensure relevance and employability.

Step 2: Certified Trainers & Assessors

Qualified professionals with industry experience and certification deliver high-quality training programs.

Step 3: Placement / OJT Support

Direct placement assistance and on-the-job training opportunities to ensure smooth transition to employment.

Step 4: Entrepreneurship Mentorship

Guidance and support for starting own business and becoming self-employed for sustainable livelihood.

Step 5: Reporting & Impact Monitoring

Continuous tracking of training outcomes and employment success to measure program effectiveness.
