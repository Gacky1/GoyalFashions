Admission Process

Step 1: Application

Submit online application with required documents and academic transcripts through our admission portal.

Step 2: Entrance Test

Appear for entrance examination or interview based on the program requirements and eligibility criteria.

Step 3: Merit List

Merit list publication based on entrance test scores, academic performance, and other selection criteria.

Step 4: Counseling

Personal counseling session to discuss program details, career prospects, and course selection guidance.

Step 5: Enrollment

Complete enrollment process with fee payment, document verification, and orientation program attendance.
