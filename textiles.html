Textiles Division

Crafting Excellence in Every Thread

We are clothing manufacturer managed by technically qualified professionals, being part of the flagship company called Goyal Fashions. The promoters have the background of extensive experience in the field of textiles & clothing business spanning over 4 decades. Our organization has been a leading manufacturer/exporter for the last over 25 years, we have established a name for ourselves in the international market for quality and reliability. The group turnover of the company is about US$ 10 million, in which about 2.5 million contributes to garment business.
